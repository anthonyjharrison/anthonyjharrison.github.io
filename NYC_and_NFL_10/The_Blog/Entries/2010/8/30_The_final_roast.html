new york and newfoundland

 
 

As our amazing time in New York comes to a close we’ve collated our notes and are please to reveal our top 5 coffee spots. We’re pretty confident that each of these cafe’s could hold their own in Melbourne!

1st - 9th Street Espresso
2nd - Stumptown
3rd - Blue Bottle
4th - Cafe Grumpy
5th - Abraco

Given we’d all seen the tourists sites on previous trips we throughly enjoyed seeing it from a new light and spending time in Williamsburg. New York is a great city and something that we’ll hopefully all visit again soon.

With the customary teary farewell in tow (saying goodbye to Andrew and Mariann) we ventured north to take our second island destination - Newfoundland!
