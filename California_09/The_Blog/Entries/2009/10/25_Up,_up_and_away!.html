What better way to enjoy the Californian wine country than via the tranquillity of a hot air balloon? Pity that now neither of us have any eyebrows - I think our face cream contains alcohol!


All good things seem to require an early rise and hot air ballooning is no different. After a 5:30am start we were briskly on our way to our launch site and before we knew it up in the air enjoying the sights of the Napa Valley. Such an amazing experience - don’t forget to check photos in our gallery.


The afternoon was even more stressful taking in great wineries such as Silverado, Opus One, O’Briens and Taittinger. Following some advice from locals we stopped off at an amazing lunch spot called Bouchon Bakery. We can understand why the Naparians love it as it gave some of the French patisseries we visited last year a run for their money.


Time to head to bed and dream of chocolate macaroons!

Sunday, 25 October 2009
