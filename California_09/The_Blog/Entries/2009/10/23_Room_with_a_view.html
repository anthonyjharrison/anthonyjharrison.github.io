As we woke up to a slightly over cast morning the day seemed to present only one option - time to shop online for prospective engagement rings!


Thankfully, for the sake of a certain person’s bank account, the cloud burnt off and the weather became perfect. We revised our plans and booked a boat cruise across Lake Tahoe taking in the major sight of yesterday - Emerald Bay. In conjunction with the bay we took a sneak peek at some of the multi million dollar mansions scattered across the shore - certainly impressive and great to get a few ideas should we ever win the lottery.


The cruise presented some breathtaking views and a chance to be out on the water and enjoy this great location from another perspective!
