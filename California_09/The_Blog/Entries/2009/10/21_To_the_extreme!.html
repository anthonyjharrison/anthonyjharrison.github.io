Who would have thought on the drive here amidst the snow and fog that we would wake up the next day to perfect blue sky and sunshine?


The 6 hour drive to Lake Tahoe was jam packed with some wild weather - wind, torrential rain and capped off with a snow storm at 7000 feet! Great fun driving without chains or snow tires - thank god we are in a rental!


Lake Tahoe is an amazing place. The western half resides in the state of California and the eastern half in Nevada. This juxtaposes Californian environmental friendliness with Nevada gambling, casinos and drive through weddings! Tomorrow we’ll be tying the knot at a biodegradable chapel!


More good weather is in store so stay tuned for some great scenic vistas. In the meantime checkout out our latest photo gallery here.
