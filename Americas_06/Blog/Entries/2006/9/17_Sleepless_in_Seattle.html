Saturday, 16 September 2006

 

There is so much free Internet in Seattle it’s no wonder Tom and Meg suffered insomnia - good news that we could finally post our blog!!


Although our stay here in Seattle is short its still been a lot of fun. Friday we headed over to Redmond where Felicity shopped and I had the enjoyment of having lunch on Bill Gates. No doubt he didn’t miss that $9.95!


Today’s excitement revolved around flying to the top of the Space Needle. Although it’s nowhere near a tall as the CN Tower (1815 feet vs 605) it’s still provides just as impressive views of the city skyline. The weather provided a perfect day to take it all in as well! I’m just glad that it doesn’t have a glass floor as I left my brown undies back in Toronto!


During our quick Seattle stop over we also got to visit the first ever Starbucks store and we also caught a glimpse of the famous Seattle fish throwers at Pike Place Market.
