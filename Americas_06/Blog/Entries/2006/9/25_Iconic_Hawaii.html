Sunday, 24 September 2006

 

When you think of Hawaii you think of four things. Pina Colada’s, “Book’em Danno”, Japanese Tourists and last but not least surfing! Today we travelled to Surfing Mecca - North Shore Oahu. Here we took in three of the most infamous beaches on the island - Sunset, Waimea Bay and Pipeline. Although the surf was pretty quiet neither of us wanted to take our chances in the water. I doubt our travel insurance would cover any North Shore surfing injuries!


Our other highlight of the day is that at our lunch spot we spotted the actor that plays Fernando Sucre from the TV series Prison Break. Felicity and I were both to gutless to ask for his autograph but we got to sit at the table that they dined at!
