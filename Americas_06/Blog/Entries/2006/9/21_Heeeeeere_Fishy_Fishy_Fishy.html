Wednesday, 20 September 2006

 

Bert and Ernie would be mighty jealous of the amount of sea-life we’ve seen so far in Maui. There was most certainly no need to use their infamous fish calling technique. We’ve been blessed with a whole array of fish and yesterday we even snorkeled with a Sea Turtle. All this is just off the beach in front of the apartment! Pretty crazy stuff!


We’ve been really lucky with the weather ever since our arrival on Sunday. We met up with Mum and Dad who’ve been on holiday on the islands since the previous Thursday. They’ve got a great head start on their tan but Felicity has given them a good run for their money in the last couple of days! It’s going to be a tight competition but I’m sure she’ll come through with the goods.


We’ve been staying in the new apartment that mum and dad purchased last year and it’s amazing! We’ve even giving the kiddy water slide a bit of a workout (Dad included).


The plan for the rest of the week is more tanning, snorkeling and sipping Pina Colada’s by the pool. We’ll post more photo’s of these strenuous activities once we get a chance!
