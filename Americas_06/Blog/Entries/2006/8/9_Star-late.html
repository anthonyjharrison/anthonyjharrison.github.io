Tuesday, 8 August 2006

 

Later next week Felicity and I were going to jump on a scenic rail trip from San Fran to LA. The train is called the Coast Starlight it is run by Amtrak and passes through arguably some of the most beautiful coastline of the world. The area is known as Big Sur. We purchased our tickets weeks ago and since that day I have been doing a little reading on what to expect. Lately the information i’ve uncovered has indicated that the train runs at least 10 hours late. Today as I stepped out the door of my hotel room I noticed on the front page of my complimentary copy of the San Francisco Chronicle an article discussing this major issue. One point that has hit home from the article was the fact in the last 12 months only 2% of travelers on this train have arrived on time. Well looks like we’ll be finding an alternative route to LA!!
