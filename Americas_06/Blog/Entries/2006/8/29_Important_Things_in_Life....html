Tuesday, 29 August 2006

 

... are shopping and eating, so thats what we did today!! :)


Again New York City weather wasn’t the finest but we still headed down to the Statue of Liberty on our booked tour. Its was great to be able to enter the monument although they don’t let you go any further than her feet. Apparently they haven’t let anyone up to her crown in 5 years.


Our morning consisted of a few other tourist sites including Ellis Island, Wall Street and the World Trade Centre Site.


During the afternoon we took out time heading northward back to our home base. We dropped in on a number of famous Manhattan areas. Little Italy for food and SOHO for shops!


We dined at Lombardi’s for lunch. New York’s first pizza place. Our advice - anyone that travels to New York has to go here. Best pizza I’ve ever had - sorry Da Angelo!


The afternoon proved to be an expensive one. SOHO shopping was just too good! Felicity bought another pair of shoes and I purchased yet another pair of jeans. In the spirit of the US Open which started today I’ll give you a scorecard of the shopping tally.


Felicity Roberts - 4 pairs of shoes +1 pair of jeans

Anthony Harrison - 2 pairs of shoes + 3 pairs of jeans
