Sunday, 27 August 2006

 

Today was a dark and rainy day here in New York City. But for one thing it hasn’t dampened our shopping spirit. Even at its weather worst New York is a blast. Today we headed up to the top of Rockefeller Centre. On route the weather gave us a little unexpected shower, but never-mind, at least we weren’t participating in the New York Half Marathon that was being held at the same time. Even on a bleak day the views were spectacular, and the positive was that we had the whole observation deck to ourselves! To keep dry we had to take cover in a number of shops and department stores (very bad for the budget - please send money!). Needless to say I can no longer fit everything in my bag and Felicity has yet another pair of shoes.


Tomorrow we are off to see the Statue of Liberty. Forecast is for more inclement weather but at least we’ve dug out mum and dad’s rain jackets. They proved their weight this afternoon!
