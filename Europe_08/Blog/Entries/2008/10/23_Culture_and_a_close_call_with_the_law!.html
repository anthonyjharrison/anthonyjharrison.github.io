europe 08

 

Culture and a close call with the law!

Thursday, 23 October 2008

 

Not a lot to report over the last few days - we’ve just been kicking back enjoying the sun and sights of the south of France.


A couple of highlights (or lowlights) include:


  1. 1.Felicity taking on the role of a human toilet for a family of pigeons in Antibes

  2. 2.Anthony being warned in the Picasso Museum for taking photo’s - second time so far during the trip - I’m guessing after the third I’ll be thrown in the slammer

  3. 3.Enjoyed way too much good food and wine in Nice and now have a lot less money for the rest of the trip - see the photo album for evidence.


No surprises to the fact we’re still having a blast. We’ve only got a few more days with the car - just enough to get us to Marseille in time to enjoy a couple of days eating bouillabaisse... ah I mean ... sightseeing.


It will be sad to see the little car go as it has been a great companion giving us the flexibility to do pretty much anything we want. In some respects though we won’t miss the thousand of roundabouts and the millions of mopeds. I have a great deal of respect for French motorists (and much hatred for moped riders)!
